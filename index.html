Food Bank of Central and Eastern North Carolina

The Food Bank of Central & Eastern North Carolina is a nonprofit organization that has provided food for our friends and neighbors facing hunger in 34 counties in central and eastern North Carolina for 40 years. The Food Bank works across the food system to provide access to nutritious food that nourishes families, children, seniors, and individuals. Through partnerships, education, and programs, the Food Bank empowers communities to overcome hunger, creating an environment where all North Carolinians thrive.

Project Overview

The goal is to create a contraption that motivates people to donate like the common dunking booth or coin funnel. So far in H. Engineering A we’ve all learned a little slice of website development, software engineering, electrical engineering, and mechanical engineering, so we’re combining all these skills into a collaborative project to demonstrate capability and raise money for charity. Our job is to create a website that offers some form of video entertainment in exchange for a monetary donation to our charity, so our group has decided to build a robot that sings and dances Kanye songs. When a user makes a donation and submits the request form, it triggers the python script to begin recording and activate the arduino. Then, the arduino powers and coordinates the robot's song and dance. The user then receives the video via email.
